## 3. KPI Definition & Business Rationale

**Definition:** Sum of all purchase amounts  
**Business Importance:** Measures overall revenue performance.

**Definition:** Total Sales ÷ Number of Transactions  
**Business Importance:** Indicates customer spending behavior per transaction.

**Business Importance:** Helps optimize inventory and promotional strategies.

### 3.5 Preferred Payment Method

## 4. Deep-Dive Analysis: Customer Segmentation

### 4.1 Segmentation Approach
Customers were segmented based on age into the following groups:
- 18–25
- 26–35
- 36–45
- 46+

This approach helps identify spending patterns across life stages.

---

### 4.2 Findings
- Customers aged **36–45** contribute the highest sales
- Premium spending is observed in older customer segments
- Younger customers show lower transaction values but higher frequency

---

## 5. Dashboard Design & Interactivity

### 5.1 Dashboard Components
The dashboard includes:
- KPI cards for key metrics
- Bar charts for sales by city and category
- Pie chart for payment method distribution
- Column chart for sales by age group

---

### 5.2 Interactivity
Interactive slicers allow filtering by:
- City
- Product Category
- Payment Method
- Age Group

These features enable stakeholders to explore insights dynamically.

## 6. Key Business Insights
- Electronics is the dominant revenue-driving category
- Delhi and Bangalore consistently outperform other cities
- UPI and Cash dominate payment preferences
- Age segmentation clearly differentiates spending behavior
- High-value customers can be targeted using age-based strategies

---

## 7. Business Recommendations
- Focus marketing efforts on the **36–45 age group**
- Expand Electronics category promotions
- Design city-specific campaigns for Delhi and Bangalore
- Enhance UPI-based offers to increase conversion
